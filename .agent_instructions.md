# Assistant UI/UX Rules - NeuroEngine OS

## 🚫 Forbidden UI Elements
1. **Redundant Content Navigation**: NEVER suggest or add "Next Step" or "Follow to Step X" buttons at the bottom of forms/cards within the AI Studio. The user prefers the clean, top-only navigation flow.
2. **Step-by-Step Duplication**: Navigation should remain strictly at the header level of the modules unless explicitly requested.

## ✅ Preferred Design Patterns
1. **Vertical Purity**: Keep the bottom of sections clean and dedicated to final actions (like "Confirm" or "Publish") or meta-info, avoiding intermediate flow buttons.
2. **Contrast & Tabs**: Maintain high contrast for labels and use the Card-Tabs system implemented in Phase 3.
